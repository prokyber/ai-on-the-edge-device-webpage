FAQ

Is it compatible with AI‑on‑the‑Edge‑Device?

Yes — use the ESP32‑S3 branch. See the repo and docs for flashing instructions.

What’s included in the package?

One AI‑On‑The‑Edge‑Cam board and an OV2640 camera module.

How do I power it?

Via PoE Ethernet (recommended), USB‑C, or battery. Low‑power sleep draws about 20 µA for battery‑friendly deployments.
